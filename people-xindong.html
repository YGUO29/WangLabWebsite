Xindong Song

Graduate Student

Education

  • MS, Tsinghua University, Beijing CHINA, Biology
  • BS, Tsinghua University, Beijing CHINA, Electronic Engineering

Research Interest

  • Pitch, harmonicity and music;
  • Visualization of neuronal computation events in primary sensory cortex;
  • Optics and optogenetics.

Current Projects

  • Micro-architecture and connections of “pitch area” on Marmoset:
    Apply two-photon microscopy and optogenetic tools to study the "pitch area" both anatomically and functionally on common marmoset (Callithrix jacchus)
  • A stereotaxically adjustable fast two-photon microscope, for imaging neuronal activity of cortical networks on awake primates:
    Design and realize a two-photon microscope based on acousto-optical deflector scanning. The compact design allows flexible mounting in respect to lateral oriented nature of auditory cortex.
